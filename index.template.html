Welcome to the unofficial TU/e introduction week webapp


During your introduction week you will get a chance to be introduced to the city, university and its student life. With over 100 active communities, there must be at least one where you fit in! This website will help you find the locations for the most important associations. It shows you information about the identity and locations of associations.
We wish you an amazing (schittermagische) introduction week at the TU/e and good luck with the start of your study!
(disclaimer: we are not the organizing committee nor try to represent them. They did an amazing job putting this week together for you and we would like to thank them for their hard work!)
